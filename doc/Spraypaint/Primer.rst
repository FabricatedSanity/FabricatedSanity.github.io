Primer
=======
There are many reasons for why you should prime a material before you apply different materials to it like paint. Primer works as prep work for the material,
 making it suseptibal to the effects and adhesion of the new material. There are many types of primers. There are different types for different materials. 
 You have wood. plastic, resin and so much more. Below we will review different types of primers, what material they are used on and how well they work in our experience.
 we will also discuss where one might be able to buy theese products.
